Returns & Refunds

At TITANSPORTS, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, our return policy makes it easy to get a refund or exchange.

Return Policy

  • Return Period: 14 days from delivery date
  • Condition: Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging
  • Tags: All original tags must be attached
  • Proof of Purchase: Order confirmation or receipt required

Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom or personalized items (unless defective)
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Underwear and intimate apparel
  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact our customer service via WhatsApp or email
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Receive return authorization and shipping label
  4. Package the item securely in original packaging
  5. Ship the item using the provided label
  6. Receive confirmation once return is received

Refund Process

  • Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days of return approval
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • You will receive email confirmation when refund is processed
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error

Exchanges

  • Exchanges are available for different sizes or colors of the same item
  • Exchange requests must be made within 14 days of delivery
  • Customer is responsible for return shipping costs
  • Exchanges are subject to availability

Defective or Damaged Items

  • If you receive a defective or damaged item, contact us immediately
  • Provide photos of the defect or damage
  • We will provide a full refund or replacement at no additional cost
  • Return shipping is prepaid for defective items

Wrong Item Received

  • If you receive the wrong item, contact us within 48 hours
  • We will arrange for the correct item to be sent to you
  • Return shipping for the wrong item is prepaid
  • Expedited shipping will be provided for the replacement

Return Shipping Costs

  • Customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error
  • Return shipping costs are deducted from the refund amount
  • We provide prepaid shipping labels for defective or wrong items
