Shipping Information

At TITANSPORTS, we strive to provide fast and reliable shipping to get your sports gear to you as quickly as possible. Here's everything you need to know about our shipping policies.

Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

Standard Shipping

Delivery Time: 3-5 business days

Cost: KSH 300

Available: Nationwide

Express Shipping

Delivery Time: 1-2 business days

Cost: KSH 600

Available: Major cities (Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru)

Free Shipping

Delivery Time: 5-7 business days

Cost: FREE

Available: Orders over KSH 5,000

Order Processing Time

  • Orders are processed within 1-2 business days
  • Custom or personalized items may take 3-5 additional business days
  • Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the next business day
  • You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships

Shipping Destinations

  • We ship to all locations within Kenya
  • International shipping is not currently available
  • Some remote areas may have extended delivery times

Package Tracking

  • All orders include tracking information
  • Track your package using the link provided in your shipping confirmation email
  • Contact our customer service if you have tracking issues

Delivery Instructions

  • Provide accurate and complete delivery address
  • Include a contact phone number for delivery coordination
  • Someone must be available to receive the package
  • We are not responsible for packages left unattended

Shipping Issues

  • If your package is delayed beyond the estimated delivery time, please contact us
  • Lost or damaged packages will be replaced or refunded
  • Incorrect delivery addresses may result in additional shipping fees
